Pumpkin Muffins

Description

This is one of my favorite recipes. This was the first set of muffins I learnt how to make. They are sweet, savoury, soft and tender with a satisfying texture. I would love to share this with all of you and I do hope you enjoy.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking powder and baking soda
  • Salt
  • Spices including cinnamon, nutmeg and ginger
  • Granulated sugar
  • Vegetable oil
  • Canned pumpkin (Fresh puree works too)
  • Eggs
  • Apple juice

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Line a 12 cup-muffin pan with paper liners.
  2. In a large mixing bowl whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg and ginger for 20 seconds. Make a well in the center of the mixture and set aside.
  3. In a separate mixing bowl whisk together sugar, oil, pumpkin, eggs and apple juice until well blended.
  4. Pour pumpkin mixture into flour mixture and fold with a rubber spatula just until combined (batter may be slightly lumpy).
  5. Divide batter among prepared muffin cups filling each nearly full.
  6. Bake in preheated oven until toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 20-25 minutes.
  7. Let the muffins cool in the pan for several minutes then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely. Store in an airtight container.

You may add chocolate chips if preferable.
