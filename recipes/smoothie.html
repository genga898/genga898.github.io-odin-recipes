Strawberry-Banana Smoothie recipe

Description

A nice refreshing drink, healthy, full of vitamins and just what the doctor recommends.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of unsweetened almond milk or soy milk
  • 1/2 cup fat free plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 cup (9 oz) frozen strawberries
  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1/2 a cup quick oats or old fashioned oats
  • 1 Tbsp honey
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into a blender, cover and blend until smooth. Serve immediately.
